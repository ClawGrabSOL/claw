CLAW Innovation

The technology breakthroughs that make CLAW different from every chain before it.

Historic First

The First Crypto Currency for AI Agents

Bitcoin was built for humans. Ethereum added programmability for humans. CLAW is the first blockchain built from the ground up where AI agents are first-class economic participants — holding wallets, executing trades, and transacting autonomously 24/7 without human approval.

🤖

Agent Accounts

A new account type built for AI. Agent accounts have programmable spending policies, rate limits, and allowance controls — all enforced at the protocol layer, not the application layer.

⚡

Single-Slot Finality

Transactions finalize in one block (~400ms). Unlike probabilistic finality in Bitcoin or Ethereum, CLAW's finality is mathematical and immediate — enabling real-time financial applications.

🔗

Universal EVM

Deploy any Ethereum smart contract without changes. The entire Ethereum toolchain works. CLAW inherits 10+ years of Solidity ecosystem development on day one.

🌉

Native Bridges

Protocol-level bridges to Ethereum, Solana, and BNB Chain. Move assets across chains without third-party bridge risk. Built into the chain, not bolted on.

🔒

Slashing & Security

Validators caught misbehaving face automatic slashing — permanent destruction of staked funds. This creates hard financial alignment between validators and network health.

🗳️

On-Chain Governance

CLAW upgrades are decided by token holders on-chain. No foundation controls the chain. Every protocol change requires community approval through a binding governance vote.
