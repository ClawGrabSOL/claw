⚠️ Avoid Scams

Protect yourself. The CLAW team will never ask for your private keys or seed phrase. Ever.

🔴 Official warning: There are no CLAW giveaways. There are no "double your CLAW" offers. Anyone claiming to be from the CLAW team and asking for funds is a scammer.

Common Scam Types

🎁

Fake Giveaways

"Send 1 CLAW, get 2 back." This is always a scam. 100% of the time. No exceptions. CLAW will never run a send-to-receive giveaway.

🎭

Impersonation

Scammers create fake social accounts, fake Telegram groups, and fake websites pretending to be official CLAW. Always verify URLs carefully. Bookmark claw.org.

🔑

Seed Phrase Theft

No legitimate service, wallet, or team member will ever ask for your 12/24-word seed phrase. If someone asks for it, they're stealing your funds.

📧

Phishing Emails

Fake emails claiming your wallet is compromised or you need to "verify" your account. The real claw.org will never email you asking for sensitive info.


The Golden Rules

  • ✅ Only use official links from claw.org
  • ✅ Never share your seed phrase with anyone — not even "support"
  • ✅ Double-check wallet addresses before sending
  • ✅ If it sounds too good to be true, it is
  • ✅ CLAW transactions are irreversible — verify before you send
  • ❌ Never click links in unsolicited DMs
  • ❌ Never connect your wallet to unknown sites
