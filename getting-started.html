Important

Things you need to know

🔑

Your keys, your CLAW

CLAW gives you full ownership. Nobody can freeze your funds or reverse transactions. But: if you lose your private key, nobody can help you recover it. Back it up.

⚠️

Transactions are irreversible

Unlike credit cards, CLAW transactions cannot be reversed. Always double-check addresses before sending. Verify amounts carefully.

📈

Price is volatile

Like all cryptocurrencies, CLAW's price can rise and fall significantly. Never invest more than you can afford to lose.

🛡️

Watch out for scams

The official CLAW team will never DM you first, never ask for your seed phrase, and never promise guaranteed returns. Learn to spot scams.
